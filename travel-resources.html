Essential Guides & Information

Expert advice to make your travel experience smooth and worry-free.

✅ Pre-Flight Checklist

  • ✔ Passport & Visas: Ensure passport is valid for at least 6 months beyond travel date.
  • ✔ Travel Insurance: Keep a printed or easily accessible offline copy.
  • ✔ Medications: Carry in original packaging with doctor's prescription.
  • ✔ Universal Adapter: Don't get caught without a charge for your devices.
  • ✔ Local Currency: Carry small bills for initial tips & taxis upon arrival.

🧳 Standard Baggage Rules

Always verify with your specific airline, but keep these international standards in mind:

  • Checked Bag: Typically 23kg (Economy) or 32kg (Business/First).
  • Cabin Bag: Usually 7kg, must fit in overhead compartments.
  • Liquids (Carry-on): Max 100ml containers placed inside a clear 1-liter ziplock bag.
  • Power Banks: Must be in hand carry ONLY (never checked in).
